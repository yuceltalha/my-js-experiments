Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup whole white flour
  • 3 tablespoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • cooking spray

Directions

  1. Step

    2. Combine milk, eggs, all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, baking powder, vegetable oil, sugar, honey, vanilla extract, and salt in an electric blender; blend until smooth.

  2. Step

    3. Place a skillet or griddle over medium heat until hot, 2 to 3 minutes. Spray with nonstick cooking spray.

  3. Step

    4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat. Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto the griddle and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 3 to 4 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 337 calories; protein 11.6g; carbohydrates 52.1g; fat 10.1g; cholesterol 82.2mg; sodium 1180.9mg.
